Vanilla Chai Protein Shake

Description

This delicious vanilla chai protein shake is the perfect treat after the gym!

    Ingredients

  • 1 scoop of vanilla protein powder
  • 1/2 cup of chai tea chilled
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinamon
  • 1/2 cup of milk of choice
  • 1 cup ice
    Steps

  1. Add ice and liquid ingredients into your shaker bottle. Add dry incredients on top and SHAKE!!!!
