Swedish Pancakes

Description

Swedish pancakes are not crepes! Everyone keeps telling me they are, but they ARE NOT.

Give them a try and see for yourself!

    Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 3 cups milk
  • 3 tbsp butter melted
  • 1 1/2 cups sifted flour
  • 1 tsp salt
    Steps

  1. Beat eggs with the milk. Add flour and salt. Mix.
  2. Wipe griddle with butter or oil. Pour thin layer of batter in pan making sure to spread all over. When edges start to bubble, it's ready to flip!
  3. Serve with desired toppings.
